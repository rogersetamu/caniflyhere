About

CanIFlyHere.us is a planning and awareness tool that checks a location against selected FAA geospatial layers. It is not flight approval. Always verify with official FAA sources and follow all applicable rules.

If you find an issue or want a feature, contact: support@caniflyhere.us (set this up later, but it looks legit for AdSense).
