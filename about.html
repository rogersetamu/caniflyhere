Drone airspace awareness using FAA data (planning tool)

About

CanIFlyHere.us is a free planning resource that overlays publicly available Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) geospatial data onto an interactive map to help recreational drone pilots visualize controlled airspace and special use areas before flight.

The tool draws from the FAA UAS Facility Map, national security flight restriction zones (both permanent and part‑time), and other open datasets to give you a quick Do not fly, Authorization likely, or OK verdict at a selected point. It does not include temporary flight restrictions (TFRs) or local ordinances.

This application does not grant flight authorization and does not replace compliance requirements. Always consult official FAA sources and local authorities before operating a drone.

Feedback and suggestions are welcome via the project’s GitHub repository.
