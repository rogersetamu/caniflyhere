Privacy Policy

CanIFlyHere.us does not store or transmit any personally identifiable information. When you enter coordinates or click “Use my location,” your location data is processed entirely in your browser and is used only to display a map and query FAA geospatial layers. We do not log coordinates or associate them with any user.

Advertising partners (for example, Google) may use cookies or similar technologies to deliver ads if and when advertising is enabled on the site. You can manage ad personalization through your Google account settings or opt out using your browser’s privacy controls.

By continuing to use this site you consent to the limited use of cookies described above. If you have questions about how your data is handled, please open an issue on the project’s GitHub repository.
